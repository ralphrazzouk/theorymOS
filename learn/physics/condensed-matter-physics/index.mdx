# Condensed Matter Physics

Condensed matter physics explores the physical properties of matter in its solid and liquid states, including their electronic, magnetic, and structural properties.

## Topics

- Crystal Structure
- Band Theory
- Semiconductor Physics
- Superconductivity
- Phase Transitions
- Many-Body Physics
- Quantum Hall Effects
