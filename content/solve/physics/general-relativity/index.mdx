# General Relativity

General relativity is Einstein's theory of gravitation, describing gravity as a geometric property of spacetime.

## Topics

- Special Relativity
- Lorentz Transformations
- Four-Vectors
- Minkowski Spacetime
- Tensor Analysis
- Covariant and Contravariant Components
- Metric Tensor
- Manifolds
- Tangent Vectors
- Curved Spacetime
- Geodesics
- Curvature Tensor
- Riemann Tensor
- Christoffel Symbols
- Principle of Equivalence
- Time Dilation
- Local Lorentz Invariance
- Einstein's Field Equations
- Einstein's Tensor
- Energy-Momentum Tensor
- Schwarzschild Solution
- Black Holes
- Gravitational Waves
- Kerr Solution
- Black Hole Thermodynamics
- Hawking Radiation
