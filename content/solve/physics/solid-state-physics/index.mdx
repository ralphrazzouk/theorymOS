# Solid State Physics

Solid state physics investigates the properties and behavior of matter in its solid phase, including crystalline and amorphous materials.

## Topics

- Crystal Structure
- Lattice Vibrations
- Phonons
- Electronic Band Structure
- Brillouin Zones
- Semiconductors
- Metals and Insulators
- Superconductivity
- Magnetism
- Ferromagnetism
- Antiferromagnetism
- Ferrimagnetism
- Optical Properties
- Dielectric Properties
- Transport Properties
- Quantum Hall Effect
- Surface Physics
- Point Defects
- Dislocations
- Grain Boundaries
- Phase Transitions
- Critical Phenomena
- Quantum Wells
- Quantum Wires
- Quantum Dots
