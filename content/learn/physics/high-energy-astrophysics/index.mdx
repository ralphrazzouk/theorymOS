# High Energy Astrophysics

High energy astrophysics investigates the most energetic phenomena in the universe, from black holes to cosmic rays.

## Topics

- Active Galactic Nuclei
- Gamma-Ray Bursts
- Relativistic Jets
- X-Ray Binary Systems
- Supernova Remnants
- Cosmic Ray Acceleration
- Accretion Disks
- Black Hole Physics
- Gravitational Wave Sources
- Pulsar Physics
- Magnetar Phenomena
- Blazar Emissions
- Relativistic Plasma Physics
- High-Energy Particle Acceleration
- Astrophysical Shock Waves
- Synchrotron Radiation
- Compton Scattering
- Radiative Transfer
- Magnetic Field Effects
- Neutrino Astronomy
