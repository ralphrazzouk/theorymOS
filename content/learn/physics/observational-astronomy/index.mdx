# Observational Astronomy

Observational astronomy focuses on the techniques and instruments used to study celestial objects and phenomena.

- Astronomical Surveys
- Exoplanet Detection

- Solar Observations
- Galactic Astronomy
- Extragalactic Astronomy
- Cosmological Observations

- Astronomical Catalogs
