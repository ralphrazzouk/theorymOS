# Stellar Evolution

Stellar evolution explores the life cycles of stars, from their formation to their final states, and the physical processes that drive their evolution.

## Topics

- Star Formation
- Protostellar Phase
- Main Sequence Stars
- Stellar Structure
- Nuclear Fusion
- Stellar Atmospheres
- Red Giants
- White Dwarfs
- Neutron Stars
- Black Holes
- Supernovae
- Binary Systems
- Mass Transfer
- Stellar Populations
- Chemical Evolution
- Stellar Winds
- Pulsating Stars
- Variable Stars
- Stellar Rotation
- Stellar Magnetic Fields
- Stellar Seismology
- Star Clusters
- Galactic Evolution
- Nucleosynthesis
- Stellar Remnants
